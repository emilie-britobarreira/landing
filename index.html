What is Flaties?

Flaties is the app that will make flat-sharing easier and more friendly.
The app offers you several different functionalities, such as push notifications, a joint account and to-do lists.
Flaties offers two types of accounts: the Standard and the Premium for customers; in addition to the Premium group offer for student residence organizations.
